Restoration

Here you will find advice on how to restore different types of vehicles and what to look for when starting your own restoration process. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced restorer, our tips and guides will help you bring your vehicle back to its former glory.


Restoring a car involves meticulous planning, research, and patience, focusing on creating a realistic budget and timeline, documenting every step with photos and labels, addressing rust immediately, and tackling the project in manageable phases (like interior, then mechanical, then body/paint) to avoid overwhelming yourself, while knowing when to call in a pro for complex jobs like spray painting.

Start by researching your specific vehicle model to understand its history, common issues, and sourcing parts. Create a detailed plan that outlines the restoration process, including timelines and budgets. Document every step with photos and labels to keep track of progress. Address rust issues promptly to prevent further damage. Tackle the restoration in manageable phases, focusing on one area at a time to avoid feeling overwhelmed. Finally, recognize when a task is beyond your skill level and don't hesitate to seek professional help for complex jobs like spray painting.
