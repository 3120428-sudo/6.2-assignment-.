About Jack

Jack is a passionate car enthusiast with over 15 years of experience in vehicle restoration. His journey began in his teenage years when he restored his first classic car, igniting a lifelong passion for bringing vintage vehicles back to life. Jack's expertise spans various types of vehicles, from classic cars to motorcycles, and he has a deep understanding of the restoration process, including sourcing rare parts and applying authentic techniques. Through this website, Jack aims to share his knowledge and inspire others to embark on their own restoration projects.
